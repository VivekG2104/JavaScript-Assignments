NASHIK

My adventures in Nashik


I recently fulfilled my dream of visiting Nashik! This fascinating city is a truly a waterfall of extremes.

Nashik is a beautiful city in Maharashtra that offers serenity at its best and is a culturally rich destination.There are various waterfalls near Nashik that will rejuvenate your soul. These waterfalls are situated in exotic locations as they originate from Brahmagiri hill and Trimbakeshwar hill of the Sahyadri Mountain Ranges.

Some highlights from my trip:

  • Vihigaon waterfall is a picturesque waterfall near Nashik, which drops from a 120-ft height from the Sahyadri Mountain Ranges. This waterfall looks amazing even from a distance.



  • Dugarwadi waterfall is a mind-blowing waterfall that drops from an altitude of 450-ft on the Sahyadri Mountain Ranges. They look very attractive during the monsoon rains. Milk color like water flows throughout the monsoon season.



  • Someshwar waterfall is a must-visit waterfall for those who take a pilgrimage to Someshwar Temple. Yet, this waterfall is named after this temple due to nearby locations.



Pune

Fort in Pune

I recently fulfilled my dream of visiting Pune! This fascinating city is a truly a fort of extremes.

Pune, located in the state of Maharashtra, India, is known as the "Queen of the Deccan". It is the cultural capital of the Maratha peoples and has a rich historical past as the former capital of the Bhonsle Marathas.

Some highlights from my trip:

  • Sinhgad Fort - Situated around 36 km southwest of Pune city, this fort is nestled on the Bhuleshwar range of the Sahyadris. Apart from its impeccable landscapes, the renowned fort of Sinhagad is also known for its rich history which will take you back to the ancient era.


  • Purandar Fort - Located around 50 Km away from Pune, Purandar Fort is famous as the birthplace of Shivaji’s son, Sambhaji. Purandar fort is a 2-storeyed building- the upper portion is known as Balekilla and the lower portion is called machi.


  • Lohagad Fort - A famous historic structure perched at an altitude of 3400 feet, Lohagad Fort is about 52 Km away from Pune. This elegant pick from the list of forts in Pune stands tall at the top of a mesmerizing hill near Malavali.
