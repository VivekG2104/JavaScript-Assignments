Vihigaon Waterfall

This is a picturesque waterfall near Nashik, which drops from a 120-ft height from the Sahyadri Mountain Ranges. This waterfall looks amazing even from a distance. The unique thing about this waterfall is that 200 people can swim at a time in its cascade during the monsoon season. This is a high drop Plunge waterfall. You must be careful during the rainy season and avoid swimming and taking a bath under this waterfall. Yet, the nature-lovers will have a good time hiking this rainforest to reach the base of this waterfall.
The people interested in photography have excellent natural vistas to take from this waterfall location. A day trip is necessary to reach this waterfall sight and see its attractions. You can feel a cool breeze before you reach the base of this waterfall with splashing sounds. It is an ideal place for honeymoon couples to spend their privacy.


Distance From Nashik: 58 Kms
Entry Fee: Nil
Timing: 09:00 AM to 05:00 PM. You must check with the Maharashtra Tourism department during the monsoon season.
Tips: You must bring hiking and trekking shoes and sticks if you seek an adventure trip to Vihigaon Falls. You must carry a water-resistant photo camera, an additional dress, and a binocular.

Someshwar Waterfall

This is a must-visit waterfall for those who take a pilgrimage to Someshwar Temple. Yet, this waterfall is named after this temple due to nearby locations. Most of the pilgrims do take a dip in this waterfall cascade and visit this temple. This is a vast 10-meter waterfall dropping from a wide rocky terrain with a single drop. This waterfall is a danger to swim or to take a bath during the rainy season from June to September.
The tourists can swim and enjoy boating on its down streams from October to January. You must hike in the lush green surroundings to reach this waterfall. This is the best place to relax and enjoy Mother Nature by the city dwellers. You can breathe fresh air and swim in the streams of the waterfall, which are with mineral values. A day trip is necessary to enjoy this waterfall and exotic surroundings.


Distance From Nashik: 11 Kms
Entry Fee: Nil
Timing: 06:00 AM to 06:00 PM
Tips: You must carry a pair of hiking shoes, jerkins, a water-resistant photo camera, an additional dress, and a binocular. It is advisable to bring enough drinking water and food too.

Dugarwadi Waterfall

This is a mind-blowing waterfall that drops from an altitude of 450-ft on the Sahyadri Mountain Ranges. They look very attractive during the monsoon rains. Milk color like water flows throughout the monsoon season. It is also the best time to visit the Dugarwadi waterfall. You must hike in the rainforest cover of the Sahyadri ranges and reach the base of this waterfall. You can swim in its down-streams when the water flow is normal. Yet, it is not advisable to directly take a bath under this waterfall and swim in its cascade during the monsoon season.
This is because you cannot expect when flooding will happen. This waterfall is present in one of the scenic wilderness locations of the Sahyadri Mountain Ranges. You can just relax with Mother Nature and enjoy the drop from a huge height with splashing water sound. This is also the best hydro attraction to visit by bird and nature lovers. This scenic waterfall is the best to take photos during the rainy months from June to September.


Distance From Nashik: 37 Kms
Entry Fee: Nil
Timing: open to the public and foreign tourists 24-hours
Tips: Adventure seekers must carry hiking, trekking shoes, and sticks. It is advisable to carry energy foods and bottled drinking water. You must not forget to bring your water-resistant photo camera and a binocular.
