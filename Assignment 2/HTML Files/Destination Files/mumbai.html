Mumbai

Juhu Beach

Juhu Beach represents the spirited vibe of the city of Mumbai.Facing the Arabian Sea, Juhu Beach ranks among the most visited beaches in Mumbai and is often seen in Bollywood movies.Situated in the posh suburban mangrove of Juhu, the beach is not only there for tourists but is also loved by the residents as well.


Things to Do at Juhu Beach

Street Food: Well, let’s focus on the culinary front there; frankly, Juhu Beach is a heaven for food enthusiasts.From pani puri and pav bhaji to bhel puri, the stalls located at Navi Mumbai’s beach are among the best street food stalls in the city.
Sunset Views:Sunset is one of the most eye-catching attractions that people would like to enjoy, mostly based on the sandy beach.This needs a walk of the sandy belts during evening hours.
Recreational Activities:You can easily go horse riding, volleyball, or just flying kites, etc.
Bollywood Glimpses:Juhu has become a residential area for many Bollywood stars, so it is not a surprise to come across some actors playing volleyball on the sand or simply walking.


Best Time to Visit

In the evening one can find a lot of liveliness, but early morning is best suited for a walk.

Marine Drive

Although not a physical beach, the promenade at Mumbai’s famous Marine Drive should be mentioned as it provides a great view of the coastline.It’s a famous curved spine shaped like a necklace if observed at night when it’s lit up along the Arabian Sea.This place is much loved by the couples, the sportsmen, and anyone in need of leisure time.


Things to Do at Marine Drive

Chowpatty Beach: This one is at one end of the Marine Drive, particularly famous for its snacks, sev puri and kulfi.
Night Views: The drive on Marine Drive at night is a beautiful sight as seen from the glittering lights.
Cultural Festivals: Several processions and festivities take place on the promenade, especially during Ganesh Chaturthi.


Best Time to Visit

Evening time is ideal for the cool wind as well as the energetic environment at night.

Versova Beach

In the recent past, Versova Beach has transformed in a big way, largely due to huge cleanup operations spearheaded by social workers and enthusiastic volunteers. Versova, which is close to Juhu Beach today, has become cleaner and friendlier to tourists and investor-friendly with both natural and cultural attractions.


Things to Do at Versova Beach

Olive Ridley Turtles: You can be fortunate to see these turtles, especially if it is a nesting time of the year.
Fishing Community: There is a fishing village in Versova that gives visitors a good view of fishermen in the region.
Yoga and Fitness: Beside it, the beach provides the persons who are fitness freaks and indulged in practicing yoga classes and jogging.


Best Time to Visit

If you prefer a less crowded area, go on weekdays; if you want something more vibrant, go during the weekend.
